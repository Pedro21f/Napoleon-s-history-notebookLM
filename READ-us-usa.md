#### Prompt 2 — Summary

**Question:**
> "Summarize the previous answer in bullet points, maximum 10 lines."

**Answer obtained:**

- **Corps System:** Division of the Grande Armée into semi-autonomous units for speed and flexibility.
- **Speed and Mobility:** Dispersed advance to "live off the land" and rapid concentration.
- **Decisive Battle:** Seeking a single annihilating confrontation.
- **Envelopment Maneuvers:** Surrounding the enemy (e.g., Ulm Campaign).
- **Decoys and Counterattacks:** Luring the enemy into vulnerable positions (Austerlitz).
- **Massive Artillery Use:** Grand Battery to break enemy lines.
- **Relentless Pursuit:** Annihilating defeated remnants.

**Learning:** Requesting a specific format (bullet points, line limit) improves usability.

#### Prompt 3 — Glossary

**Answer obtained:**

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Grande Armée** | Napoleon's reorganized, highly efficient army |
| **Corps System** | Autonomous units of 15-30K soldiers |
| **Continental System** | Economic blockade against Britain |
| **Guerrilla Warfare** | Ambush-based resistance (Spain and Portugal) |
| **Scorched Earth** | Russian tactic of destroying resources |

---

#### Prompt 4 — Strategic Mistakes

**Question:**
> "What were Napoleon's main strategic mistakes that led to his fall?"

**Answer obtained:**

- **The "Spanish Ulcer":** Underestimated Spanish nationalism, leading to guerrilla warfare.
- **Russian Disaster (1812):** Logistical failure; scorched earth decimated the Grande Armée.
- **Continental System Failure:** Unenforceable blockade forced ruinous invasions.
- **Arrogance:** Hesitation in Moscow and rejection of peace proposals.
- **Nepotism:** Incompetent brothers on European thrones.
